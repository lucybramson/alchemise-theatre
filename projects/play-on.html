Play On!

Adult drama and creative play sessions for improved mental health.

Is the grind of adult life bringing you down? Is your inner child feeling neglected? Come down one evening for two hours of low pressure drama games, storytelling and creative play, and leave with a belly full of laughter.

Sessions will be led by Louise who has 15 years experience of facilitating projects for communities, and has witnessed the transformative power of theatre and play on everyday mental health.

All adults welcome, of all ages and experience. Please enquire about access requirements.

Contact us at alchemisetheatre@gmail.co.uk to book or go to our socials to find details.
