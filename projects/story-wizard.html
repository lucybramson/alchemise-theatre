Story Wizard

Where a magical spell will transform your drawings into a story adventure!

Come and draw your favourite character, your most enthralling location, and a mystical magical object. The Story Wizard will select your drawings from his Story Hat, cast his whimsical Story Spell and an improvised adventure will appear before your very eyes!

A family show for all ages. Available for touring at festivals, theatre spaces, schools, hospitals, birthday parties and anywhere that wants us! 

Actors and musicians involved: Neil Frost, Dan Lees, Annie Bashford  Louise Dickinson, Evie Fehily.

Please contact us at alchemisetheatre@gmail.co.uk to find out about pricing and book.
